## ğŸ§  What is PolicyGuard AI?

PolicyGuard AI is an intelligent compliance monitoring system that eliminates the overhead of manual policy audits. It ingests unstructured policy documents (PDFs), uses large language models to extract structured, machine-readable rules, and then continuously scans your organization's data to detect violations â€” with clear, explainable justifications for every flag raised.

> **The problem it solves:** Compliance teams spend thousands of hours manually reviewing transactions, employee records, and operational data against policy documents that are dense, ambiguous, and constantly changing. PolicyGuard AI automates this end-to-end.
